    what's dosha?
    dosha is a first of its kind, ready-to-drink, authentic chai latte. We blend authentic flavors, functional ingredients and less sugar into a delicious beverage.

    what does dosha mean?


    we draw from the ayurvedic concept of doshas, which are the forces that make up our physical bodies. there are three doshas, and we are at our best when all are doshas are balanced. the doshas are:
    • vāta, or air
    • pitta, or heat
    • kapha, or water.
    Balance your dosha with our functional chai lattes
